Thank you to everyone who attended the Chamber's Annual Meeting that was held March 10 2011 at Sea Trail Golf Resort & Convention Center.  

This year's event was based on 35 years of reflections which embraces the past, present and future of the Brunswick County Chamber of Commerce.
  

To move the Chamber forward we have developed a new logo, mission statement and tagline.  The focus is on building the community and supporting businesses. The new tagline and mission statement support these concepts and is essential for future growth.

 

The new tagline: Building Community and Supporting Business.


The new mission statement: The Brunswick County Chamber of Commerce is a membership driven organization, striving to enhance and promote Brunswick County through building community and supporting businesses.


Our members, employees and board of directors connect with what the brand stands for -- quality, innovation, and values-rich in culture. Today, we're strengthening all our attributes and giving our brand a look that is more relevant for business today and also represents Brunswick County.
