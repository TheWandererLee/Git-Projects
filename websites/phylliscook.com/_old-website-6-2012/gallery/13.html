Patient Gallery



 
 
This patient was referred to Dr. Cook’s office in extreme discomfort due to an infection. Dr. Cook thought the infection was because of the need for a root canal on his lower left front tooth. Even after the root canal, the patient continued to have infection. She performed flap surgery and cleaned out the infection. Due to the significant destruction of bone caused by the infection, bone grafting with Emdogain was completed. The healing was monitored for a year. Regeneration of the bone is excellent, as seen in the x-rays.
